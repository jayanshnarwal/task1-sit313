1. Aurdino bluetooth rover

In this project, I have made a Aurdino bluetooth rover with lights and horn. The Arduino Bluetooth rover with lights and horn is a captivating and interactive robotic marvel. Controlled effortlessly through a smartphone or computer via Bluetooth, this agile rover glides across surfaces with ease, responding promptly to users' commands. Its vibrant LED lights illuminate the path, adding a touch of enchantment to its maneuvers. Moreover, the inclusion of a lively horn provides audible amusement, making it an instant favorite among enthusiasts of all ages. This creative amalgamation of Arduino ingenuity, wireless technology, and playful accessories renders the rover an engaging and educational project that offers endless possibilities for exploration and fun. Whether it's navigating obstacle courses or delighting with its colorful display and cheerful horn, the Arduino Bluetooth rover ensures boundless entertainment and learning opportunities for aspiring inventors and tech enthusiasts alike.

2. Smart Helmet

In this project, I have made a Smart Helmet. Delivery riders face safety challenges like accidents, impaired riding, speeding, and helmet misuse. Conventional helmets fall short in addressing these concerns. Faster accident response, prevention of impaired riding, and improved accountability with advanced helmet features are essential for enhancing rider safety, particularly for those working on delivery platforms. The Smart Helmet initiative aims to improve the safety and operational effectiveness of motorcycle delivery services. It incorporates sensors such as the MAX 30100 heart rate sensor, MQ3 alcohol sensor, and Neo 8m GPS sensor to monitor the rider's health, track their whereabouts, and detect alcohol levels. This information allows for the early detection of health emergencies and effective delivery route planning.
